November Events Highlight

The Grit Hub+, Framily Friday

On 1st November, the Youth Development Division, in collaboration with The Grit Hub+, hosted Framily Friday at The GRIT Project. The event featured engaging STEM activities such as chromatography card-making, where children crafted vibrant cards for their loved ones while exploring the intersection of science and creativity. The day concluded with a heartwarming birthday celebration, fostering gratitude, family appreciation, and meaningful connections. A special thank you goes to the student volunteers from River Valley High School for their invaluable contributions in making this event a success!
