January Events Highlight

Ah Gong Ah Ma 2.0

On 18th January, the Community Service Division organised Ah Gong Ah Ma 2.0 in collaboration with Tzu Chi @ Nanyang to bring Chinese New Year joy to our beloved elderly through various cultural activities such as Fai Chun Writing & Red Packet DIY. The event was filled with joy, laughter as well as meaningful interactions and connections.


Janitor's Appreciation Project

On 18th January, the Community Service Division collaborated with NTU Muslim Society to organise an event to express our gratitude and appreciation to NTU's hardworking janitors. Our volunteers engaged with the janitors through a series of station games and a shared meal, creating a memorable moment to show appreciation to the Janitors for keeping our campus clean.


MiGreat HealthServe 3

On 18th January, the International Service hosted their MiGreat HealthServe 3 event, a community-driven initiative to support and improve the well-being of migrant workers. Our volunteers assisted with health checks, managing booths and created a caring and inclusive environment for the migrant workers.


LinkedIn Workshop

The Professional Development Division organised a Linkedin Workshop on 23rd January, participants gained valuable insights on optimising their LinkedIn profile and leverage networking strategies to advance their career from Ms Lim Mei Yu. The interactive session provided practical tips and personalised advices to the participants, which are beneficial to their job search.
