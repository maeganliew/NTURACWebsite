Divisions

Community Service

Service division




The Community Service Division stands as a beacon of hope, dedicated to making a tangible impact on our local community, in particular the elderly and janitors around our school. Our vision is to empower change through community service. Our division not only serves the community but also fosters personal growth. Members develop leadership, teamwork, and empathy by engaging with those in need. Through these experiences, we forge lasting friendships with like-minded individuals who share a passion for community service and continuously create a dynamic force for positive change.

Divisions

Youth Development

Service division




Youth Development is a division that provides a platform for Rotaractors to serve the youth community. The youths are the future of our generation and it is thus important for us to help shape the youths in our community into beings who will actively and positively impact our society. We look forward to bringing joy to children and youth in our community by providing them with support, be it academically or emotionally. In doing so, we also have a goal of personal growth as we take the journey together to positively impact the community around us. As we support them in the challenges that they face, we also seek to bring joy to those that we support and grow stronger together.

Divisions

International Service

Service division




The International Service Division at the Rotaract Club of NTU is dedicated to making a positive impact on the local migrant workers community and the underprivileged communities worldwide through our various volunteering activities and the flagship Overseas Community Engagement Project (OCEP), especially in the areas of engagement, sustainability, cross-cultural exchange, and skill development. We welcome volunteers and collaborators to join us in our mission to create lasting change and promote understanding on a global scale. Together, we strive to make the world a better place for all.

Divisions

Special Project

Service division




Special Projects is a division that focuses on enabling the individuals with disabilities. Persons With Disabilities (PWDs), are often discriminated against and have trouble in their day-to-day activities. We as a division hope to bring new experiences and impart essential life skills to the PWDs. In doing so, we hope to improve their everyday lives, and ease the burden of their caretakers. Caring for PWDs is a lifelong duty, and we welcome volunteers to join us in taking the first step on this compassionate journey of support and inclusion.

Business and Finance

Support division




The Business and Finance Division functions as a vital link connecting RACNTU with the corporate realm. We are a dynamic team responsible for securing sponsors, driving fundraising efforts, and orchestrating impactful events. We forge partnerships with local businesses and organizations who share our mission, organize events that foster collaboration and drive meaningful change. Beyond these roles, our focus extends to member development and personal growth, which then serves as a platform for building relationships, not just within our division but also in the broader landscape. As a team, we empower our members to learn, connect, and excel in their journey of impact.
