April Events Highlight

Ah Gong Ah Ma

On the 13th of April, our Community Service Division had organized their flagship event, Ah Gong Ah Ma! Our volunteers headed to Tzu Chi community centre @ Nanyang to engage with the seniors. The seniors enjoyed themselves in a game of relay, bingo and singing session, making happiness and joyous memories with our volunteers! All in all, we would like to thank Tzu Chi for this wonderful collaboration, the seniors for their enthusiasm and to our Community Service volunteers for their dedication!


Investing Workshop

NTU Rotaract Club empowers students with practical financial knowledge and skills to navigate the complex world of investing. The workshop featured experienced speakers who shared insights on a variety of investment topics, including basic principles of investing, risk assessment, portfolio diversification, and market trends. Attendees learned about different asset classes, such as stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and alternative investments, gaining an understanding of their unique characteristics and potential returns.
